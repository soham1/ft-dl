Help

This utility downloads the latest subbed Fairy Tail episodes in 720p for free! In order for this utility to work properly, download Wget. After inputting the start and end episodes, click the start button. Wait until progress bar shows the task is complete. Then, click the copy button and open your command console. Paste the command into and press enter. Wait until download finishes. (To specify where to download, just cd to your desired directory.) I have found out that some episodes are not on the website. These episodes are: 100, 101, 102, 103, 104, 112, 170. You can watch these missing episodes at Ryu Anime.
